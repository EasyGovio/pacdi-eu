5. Privacy Policy

Data Controller

AskMeAI Teknoloji Ltd. Şti. (Trade Registry: 23837), reachable at info@pacdi.eu, is the data controller for all personal data processed through PACDI-operated platforms.

Data We Collect

  • Tool usage data: Most PACDI tools store data exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage). No tool data is transmitted to our servers.
  • Early Access e-mail: If you voluntarily submit your e-mail address, it is used solely to notify you of product launches. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • PDF form assistance (wasstehtmirzu.com): Documents submitted for form-filling are processed for that sole purpose and deleted after delivery.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs are retained for up to 7 days for security purposes.

Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–21)

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. Contact info@pacdi.eu. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Transfers

AskMeAI Teknoloji Ltd. Şti. does not sell personal data. Where third-party services are used (Google Analytics, Google AdSense, EmailJS), data may be processed outside the EU/EEA under Standard Contractual Clauses.
